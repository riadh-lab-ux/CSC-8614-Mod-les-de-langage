Bonjour à tous,

Voici des sujets de PFE supplémentaires proposés par Luca Benedetto :

*#1 AI Study Companion*
Development of an app that turns static course material into an 
interactive tutor.

Description: A virtual study assistant designed to help students master 
course content. The assistant answer questions and proactively quizzes 
the student based on their lecture notes and slides, identifies 
knowledge gaps, and provides explanations sourced directly from the 
provided materials.

Collected Data: Course PDFs, lecture transcripts, student quiz 
performance, and interaction logs.

Potential Clients: University students, online education platforms (EdTech).

Models: Large Language Models (LLMs), Vector Databases, and RAG 
pipelines for grounded response generation.


*#2: Scientific Research Navigator*
Implementation of a NotebookLM-inspired application, which helps 
students and researches in navigating research papers.

Description: An application that helps students/researchers in 
synthesizing information from multiple scientific papers. The tool can 
cross-reference findings between different PDFs, summarize them and help 
draft literature reviews by citing specific sources from the uploaded 
corpus.

Collected Data: Scientific papers (ArXiv, PubMed, etc.), 
user-highlighted snippets, and research notes.

Potential Clients: Master/PhD students, academic researchers, and R&D 
departments.

Models: Large Language Models, Vector databases RAG pipeline, embedding 
models for semantic search.
